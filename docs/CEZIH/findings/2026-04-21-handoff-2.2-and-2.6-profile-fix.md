## Root hypothesis (same for both)

The delta between working and failing payloads is:

**Working (2.4/2.9)** sends: identifier + subject + clinicalStatus + abatementDateTime
**Failing (2.2/2.6)** adds: `code` (ICD), `onsetDateTime`, `asserter`, `verificationStatus`, `note`

One of those extra fields is not sliced-in on the 2.2/2.6 profile. Most likely
candidates (ranked):

1. **`asserter`** вЂ” status updates never emit it. CEZIH may forbid changing asserter.
2. **`code` (ICD)** вЂ” unchanged ICD may need to be omitted, not re-sent.
3. **`onsetDateTime`** вЂ” timezone suffix (`+02:00`) vs date-only.
4. **`clinicalStatus` CodeSystem** вЂ” we use `terminology.hl7.org/...`, CEZIH may want `fhir.cezih.hr/...`.
5. **`note.extension` annotation-type code** вЂ” we always send code=4; update may need different.
